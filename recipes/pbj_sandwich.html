PB&J sandwich Recipe

Back to index page

Description

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich (PB&J) consists of peanut butter and fruit preserves—jelly—spread on bread.

This is a good and common breakfast item.


Ingredients

  • penut butter
  • Jelly
  • Sliced Bread
  • toaster(optional)

Step to make Sandwich

  1. Have your sliced bread ready whether it be toasted or not.
  2. Spread a teaspoon of penut butter on one of the slices of bread.
  3. Spread a teaspoon of jelly on the other sliced piece of bread.
  4. Put the penut butter slice of bread and the jelly slice of bread together.
  5. Now you have your PB&J Sandwich so enjoy!
