Boiled Eggs Recipe

Back to index page

Description

Hard-boiled eggs are cooked so that the egg white and egg yolk both solidify.

This recipe will show how to utilize melted butter with salt and pepper so it can be very tasty.

Ingredients

  • melted butter
  • salt
  • pepper
  • eggs

Steps to make Boiled eggs

  1. Hard boil your eggs for about 20 minutes.
  2. Peel your eggs and then split them into quarters or halves depending on your preference.
  3. Melt butter until you can tell that it is liquidy.
  4. Now pour around a teaspoon of melted butter on each slice.
  5. Place however much salt and pepper you want on each egg slice.
  6. Your boiled eggs are now ready to eat so enjoy!
