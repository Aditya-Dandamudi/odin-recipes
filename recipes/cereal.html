Cereal Recipe

Description

Breakfast cereal, grain food, usually precooked or ready-to-eat.

Is customarily eaten with milk or cream for breakfast in the United States and elsewhere, often sweetened with sugar, syrup, or fruit.

Ingrediants

  • Cereal of your choice
  • Milk of your choice
  • Any type of Bowl
  • spoon

Steps for how to prepare your cereal.

  1. Get your bowl ready and clean.
  2. Pour in however much cereal you want.
  3. Pour in enough milk but not to the point where it out ratios the cereal.
  4. Get your spoon and enjoy your cereal.
